About the program

It's easy to earn with every friend you refer. Share your referral link with your friends and we'll gift your friends Rs 150 in MyGlammXO POINTS, which are redeemable on purchase of any MyGlamm product. If your friend has not previously registered with MyGlamm, they need to register using your referral link. When they make their first purchase, we will send you an email with a gift code worth the same product order value. You will also continue to earn cashback in MyGlammXO POINTS of up to 25% on future purchases your friend makes.
